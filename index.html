About Us

KHM ‘22

Kochi Hub Meet (KHM) is the annual flagship event of IEEE Kochi Hub, providing a platform for Student Branches all across the hub to connect, network, and learn together. Jam-packed with fun, creative, and innovative activities, KHM provides a premiere experience for its attendees in growing their technical as well as non-technical skills. Together with St. Joseph College of Engineering Palai, we are bringing up the most awaited carnival of this season.
